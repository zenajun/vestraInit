Manufacturing Specialists of Air And Liquid Filter Products since 1988

Dynamic filtration limited was established in 1988 with 8,500 sq ft of production space in waterloo, Onatrio. In 2014, we underwent an extensive building expansion which nearly doubled our production capacity. As a proud family owned & operated business, we have over 38 years of experience in the filtration industry and are a manufacturer and supplier to North America for inlet filters, and filter systems for compressors and blower units.
